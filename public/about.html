What is GameStates?

A simple site that helps you view how many active players are in your games. Let's you see whether your favorite game is alive and thriving or a dead wasteland.

How it works?

We pull the current number of monthly players using API calls to their respective games' databases giving you real time info on how many people are playing that game.

What this will tell you?

This should tell you whether you should buy/re-install a multiplayer game based on whether there is a healthy amount of current players.
